How the Lucky Mobile Number Calculator Works

Numerology is the belief that numbers carry vibrations that shape our nature and our luck. In Vedic and Chaldean numerology your ruling number (the Moolank or psychic number) comes from your date of birth, and it is governed by a planet that colours your personality, your emotions and the numbers that are lucky — or unlucky — for you.

Our calculator works in three steps:
