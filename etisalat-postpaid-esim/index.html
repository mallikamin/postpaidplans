The eSIM does not change the plan

This matters: choosing an eSIM affects only delivery, not the deal. Your price, data and minutes, free VIP number, VAT and the 12 to 24 month commitment are identical to a physical SIM. You simply skip the plastic card and the wait. Billing runs exactly as any Etisalat postpaid line.
