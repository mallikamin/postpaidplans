What makes a number premium

It comes down to memorability and rarity: repeating digits, mirrored sequences, ascending runs and culturally significant numbers stand out and stick in the mind, which is valuable for business and personal branding alike. The eSIM has no effect on a number's tier or value; it only changes how the line is delivered.
