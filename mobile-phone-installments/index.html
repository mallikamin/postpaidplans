Who can apply (eligibility, the honest version)

SmartPay is a postpaid feature, so the device installment sits on an active Etisalat postpaid line. The first requirement is not optional, and here is exactly what Etisalat looks for, with no surprises:

  • An Etisalat postpaid number is a must. SmartPay device installments are only available on an active Etisalat postpaid line, so you need a postpaid number first. If you do not have one, we open it for you and pair it with the right plan.
  • Salary above AED 4,000. You can usually apply right after taking a postpaid SIM.
  • Salary AED 4,000 or below. Clear about three months of postpaid bills first, then you become eligible. We tell you honestly where you stand and set a simple path to qualify.
  • Profile and credit check. Etisalat verifies your profile. An upfront or down payment may apply based on that check, and the first month of device rental is charged to your card at order, then monthly via Auto Pay.
  • Name and document match. The name on SmartPay must match your payment card and your Emirates ID, and both must be present at delivery.

Important. Mobile phone issuance on installments is subject to Etisalat's own internal verification and approval. As an Authorized Etisalat Dealer we open the postpaid line and submit your e& SmartPay application; the final credit decision, any down payment, and the device issuance are determined by Etisalat.

Not sure if you qualify? Send us a message and we check your eligibility for you before you commit to anything.
