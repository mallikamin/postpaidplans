Get your Etisalat eSIM, no physical SIM needed

An Etisalat (e&) eSIM is your mobile line stored digitally in your phone instead of on a plastic card. You can take a brand new Etisalat postpaid line as an eSIM with a free VIP number, or convert your existing physical SIM to eSIM in the e& UAE app. As an Authorized Etisalat Dealer we set it up for you, often the same day. Why choose an eSIM?
