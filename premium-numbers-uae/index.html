How the three premium number tiers work

Across the UAE, premium numbers — also called VIP, fancy or golden numbers — are graded by how striking their pattern is. The grading is simple once you know the logic: the more repetition or the cleaner the sequence, the higher the tier and the rarer the number. Here is the whole system at a glance.

SILVER

Silver Numbers

Light patterns — a repeating pair or short sequence. Included free with most postpaid plans from AED 188/mo.

GOLD

Gold Numbers

Stronger patterns — triples such as 777 or 888 and clean sequences. Pairs with the Gold Plan tier.

PLATINUM

Platinum Numbers

The rarest — quad repeats like 8888, mirrored digits or ascending 12345. Pairs with the Platinum Plan.
