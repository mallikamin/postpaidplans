Updated 25 May 2026 · Postpaid Plans UAE

Short answer

For most UAE residents in 2026, the Unlimited Calls 600 Flexi plan at AED 360/month is the best all-rounder — 50GB data, unlimited flexi minutes (local + international), 2GB roaming and a free Silver VIP number. If you want the cheapest entry with a free number, start with the Freedom Plan 250 at AED 188/month; for unlimited everything, the Platinum Plan at AED 1,000/month is the top tier.

Choosing an Etisalat postpaid plan in 2026 comes down to three things: how much data you actually burn through, whether you make a lot of calls (and to where), and which free VIP number tier you'd like to walk away with. Best plan by type of user

Best budget plan: Freedom Plan 250 — AED 188/mo

If you mostly use Wi-Fi at home and work and just need a reliable line with non-stop data, this is the cheapest way in. You still get 1,000 local minutes, 2GB roaming for trips, and a free Silver number. It's the natural pick for students, a second SIM, or anyone keeping costs tight.

Best all-rounder: Unlimited Calls 600 Flexi — AED 360/mo

50GB of data covers heavy daily streaming and hotspotting, and the unlimited flexi minutes mean you stop worrying about whether a call is local or international. For most professionals and families this is the plan that "just works".

Best for unlimited data without going premium: Freedom Unlimited Data 500 — AED 300/mo

Currently discounted from AED 600. If your priority is unlimited high-speed data rather than huge call allowances, this is excellent value — 5GB roaming and a free Silver number included.

Best for international callers: Unlimited 1 Country 325 — AED 325/mo

If you regularly call one specific country, this plan gives unlimited minutes to that destination plus 300 local minutes and 30GB of data — usually cheaper than buying international add-ons every month.

Best premium plan: Gold Plan 500 & Platinum Plan

The Gold Plan 500 (AED 500/mo) delivers unlimited data and unlimited local + international calls and pairs with a Gold VIP number. The Platinum Plan (AED 1,000/mo) is the flagship — unlimited everything, 20GB roaming and a rare Platinum number for those who want the strongest patterns. Learn what separates the tiers in our Silver, Gold & Platinum guide.

Best for UAE nationals: Emirati Freedom — AED 250/mo

Exclusive to UAE nationals with a valid Emirati ID: unlimited high-speed data and unlimited local calls at a flat AED 250/month.

How to actually decide

  • Light user, tight budget? Freedom Plan 250.
  • Heavy data + lots of calls? Unlimited Calls 600 Flexi.
  • Unlimited data is the priority? Freedom Unlimited Data 500.
  • Call one country a lot? Unlimited 1 Country 325.
  • Want a Gold or Platinum number? Gold Plan 500 or Platinum Plan.
