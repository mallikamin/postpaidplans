What is an eSIM?

An eSIM is a SIM built into your phone instead of a small plastic card you slot in. Your Etisalat line is downloaded onto the device as a secure digital profile, so there is no physical SIM to collect, swap or lose. On most modern phones an eSIM works exactly like a normal line: same number, same plan, same coverage. The advantages are practical. There is nothing to deliver, you can activate in minutes, and on dual-SIM phones you can run a second line (for example a work or travel line) alongside your main one.

In the UAE, Etisalat (now branded e&) offers eSIM across its postpaid plans. This guide covers which devices work, how to activate or convert step by step, and how to pair an eSIM with a plan and a free VIP number through us.

How to activate or convert to an Etisalat eSIM

The fastest route is the e& UAE app (the app formerly known as My Etisalat UAE). The same flow works whether you are activating a new eSIM or converting a physical SIM to eSIM. Do this on the phone you want the eSIM on.

  1. Open the e& UAE app and log in with Facial Recognition.
  2. Tap Manage on your line.
  3. Pick the SIM you want to convert or transfer.
  4. Open Select SIM card.
  5. Choose Replace SIM / Switch to eSIM.
  6. Set the SIM type as eSIM and follow the on-screen steps.

Once you confirm, the physical SIM loses signal first, and the eSIM activates within about 3 to 5 minutes. Prefer not to do it yourself? You can also activate at the e& website (etisalat.ae) or any e& store, and as your dealer we can walk you through it on WhatsApp.

Before you start: what you need to know

  • Be in the UAE. Activation through the app needs you to be physically located in the UAE with location enabled. A request will not go through from outside the country.
  • Log in with Facial Recognition. The app uses facial recognition (UAE Pass style identity) to authorise the SIM change.
  • 24-hour window. You must complete activation within 24 hours of the request, or it is cancelled and you start again.
  • Cost. Etisalat has offered eSIM activation free as a limited-time promotion on the e& UAE app. Promotions change, so we confirm the current fee with you before you order. There is no separate charge from us for arranging your plan and number.
  • Keep your QR or details handy. If you activate a second device by QR code, scan it with the new phone and confirm.
