How to Choose a VIP Number in Dubai

Updated 25 May 2026 · Postpaid Plans UAE

Short answer

To choose a VIP number in Dubai: (1) decide on a pattern you'll remember, (2) pick the tier that fits it — Silver, Gold or Platinum, (3) check live availability, (4) match it to the right Etisalat postpaid plan, and (5) confirm on WhatsApp for same-day SIM delivery. Silver numbers are free with plans from AED 188/mo.

A VIP number is something you'll read out hundreds of times — on calls, business cards, WhatsApp and forms. Getting it right is worth ten minutes of thought. This is the exact process we walk Dubai customers through as an Official e& Partner & Authorized Etisalat Dealer.

Step 1 — Decide what "memorable" means to you

The best VIP number is the one you find easiest to recall and dictate. Common starting points:

  • A repeating digit you like (lots of 7s or 8s).
  • A meaningful date — birthday, anniversary, a lucky number.
  • A clean ending — the last few digits are what people remember most.
  • A symmetric or sequential pattern that just "flows" when spoken.

If you're not sure what's considered desirable in the UAE, our top fancy numbers guide lists the patterns people chase most.

Step 2 — Pick your tier

Patterns map onto three tiers. This decides which plan you'll pair the number with.

SILVER

Everyday VIP

Light patterns, free with most plans from AED 188/mo. The popular choice.

GOLD

Standout

Triples and clean sequences. Pairs with the Gold Plan (AED 500/mo).

PLATINUM

Prestige

Quad repeats and mirrors. Pairs with the Platinum Plan (AED 1,000/mo).

For a full breakdown of how the tiers differ, read Silver vs Gold vs Platinum numbers.

Step 3 — Check live availability

Good numbers move quickly, so always work from a live list rather than a wishlist. Open the Choose Number page to browse current Dubai stock by tier, or start from the VIP numbers Dubai hub. Shortlist two or three you'd be happy with — your first pick may already be reserved.

Step 4 — Match the number to a plan

In the UAE a VIP number is bundled with a postpaid plan, so picking the number and picking the plan happen together. Match the tier to the right plan:

Step 5 — Confirm and get your SIM

Once you've shortlisted a number and a plan, send them to us on WhatsApp. We confirm the number is still available, lock it in, check your documents (Emirates ID, plus passport/visa for residents) and arrange same-day SIM delivery across Dubai, Abu Dhabi and Sharjah.

Quick tips before you commit

  • Say it out loud. If it's awkward to dictate over a call, skip it.
  • Mind the ending. A strong last three or four digits beats a strong middle.
  • Have a backup. Shortlist a second number in case your favourite is gone.
  • Think long term. You keep the number across plan changes, so choose one you'll be happy with for years.
