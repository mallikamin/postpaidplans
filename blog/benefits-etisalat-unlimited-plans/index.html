Etisalat Unlimited Plans 2026: Are They Worth It?

Updated 25 May 2026 · Postpaid Plans UAE

Short answer

An Etisalat unlimited plan gives you a predictable monthly bill with no data overage charges, unlimited high-speed data on the top tiers, unlimited local or flexi minutes, a roaming allowance and a free VIP number. The cheapest unlimited-data option is the Freedom Unlimited Data 500 at AED 300/mo; the best all-round value is the Unlimited Calls 600 Flexi at AED 360/mo. To compare all Etisalat postpaid plans side by side, use our free Plan Finder.

"Unlimited" is one of the most useful words on a phone bill — but only if you pick the right kind of unlimited. Etisalat offers unlimited data, unlimited local calls, unlimited flexi minutes and unlimited-to-one-country options, and each suits a different person. Here's what you actually gain, and how to match the right one to you.

The core benefits

1. A bill that never surprises you

The biggest win is predictability. With unlimited data you stop watching a counter and you never get hit with overage charges at the end of the month. For families and businesses, that single fixed line on the budget is worth a lot.

2. Genuinely heavy usage without throttling worries

Streaming, video calls, hotspotting a laptop, navigation — modern phone use eats data fast. Plans like the Freedom Unlimited Data 500 (AED 300/mo) and the Gold Plan 500 (AED 500/mo) give unlimited high-speed data so you can use your phone the way you actually want to. For a full side-by-side of every option, see our guide to the best Etisalat unlimited data plan.

3. Flexi minutes that cover local and international

Flexi minutes draw from one shared pool for both local and international calls — no juggling separate add-ons. The Unlimited Calls 600 Flexi (AED 360/mo) includes unlimited flexi minutes, which is ideal if you call abroad regularly but don't want a dedicated international package.

4. Roaming allowance included

Most unlimited plans bundle roaming data so a quick trip doesn't mean buying a travel pack. Allowances run from 2GB up to 20GB on the Platinum Plan.

5. A free VIP number

Every unlimited plan comes with a free number from the matching tier — Silver on the Freedom and Unlimited plans, Gold on the Gold Plan, Platinum on the Platinum Plan. See how the tiers work in our Silver vs Gold vs Platinum guide.

Which "unlimited" is right for you?

  • You stream and hotspot a lot, calls matter less → Freedom Unlimited Data 500 (AED 300/mo).
  • You call within the UAE constantly → Unlimited Local Calls 325 (AED 325/mo).
  • You call one specific country often → Unlimited 1 Country 325 (AED 325/mo).
  • You want it all — data and calls anywhere → Unlimited Calls 600 Flexi (AED 360/mo) or Gold Plan 500.
  • You want the flagship, no compromises → Platinum Plan (AED 1,000/mo).

When unlimited might be overkill

If you're mostly on Wi-Fi and use little mobile data, a non-stop-data Freedom plan from AED 188/mo can be cheaper than a full unlimited plan while still bundling a free Silver number.
