How Much Does a Golden Number Cost in UAE?

Updated 25 May 2026 · Postpaid Plans UAE

Short answer

In the UAE, a golden (VIP) number is included free with the matching Etisalat postpaid plan. A Silver number is free with plans from AED 188/mo; a Gold number comes with the Gold Plan at AED 500/mo; a Platinum number comes with the Platinum Plan at AED 1,000/mo. Standalone premium-number pricing varies by how rare the pattern is and is confirmed on WhatsApp.

It's the question we get asked most, and the honest answer surprises people: you usually don't pay a separate price for a golden number at all. The cost is built into the postpaid plan it pairs with. Here's exactly how the pricing works so there are no surprises.

VIP numbers are bundled with the plan

Rather than selling numbers and plans separately, Etisalat bundles a number from the matching tier into each plan. Choose the plan, and the number of that tier comes with it at no extra charge for the number itself.

What about standalone premium numbers?

Some customers want a specific rare number outside the standard plan bundle — for example a particular quad-repeat or mirror they've set their heart on. In those cases, standalone premium-number pricing exists and varies by how rare the pattern is. Because every number is unique, there's no fixed price list — the exact figure for a given number is confirmed on WhatsApp before you commit. We don't quote invented prices online, only the real, current figure for the specific number you want.

What makes one number cost more than another?

Whether bundled or standalone, value comes down to rarity. The factors that push a number up the tiers:

  • Repetition length — a pair (88) is Silver-level, a triple (888) is Gold-level, a quad (8888) is Platinum-level.
  • Sequence and symmetry — clean runs like 12345 and mirrored numbers are scarce and prized.
  • Position — a strong pattern at the end of the number is worth more than the same digits in the middle.
  • Lucky/meaningful digits — 7, 8 and patterns like 786 carry extra demand.

For most people, a golden number costs nothing beyond the plan they were going to take anyway — Silver from AED 188/mo, Gold with the AED 500/mo plan, Platinum with the AED 1,000/mo plan.
